This project is a Final Component Output made by students from De La Salle University - Manila.

The team’s approach in presenting the topic, "The Challenges of the Education System During the Spanish Colonial Rule", is through this website named The Legacy of Rizal and the Education System.

The goal is to provide useful insights into the political landscape, cultural influences, and economic prospects of the 19th century Philippines in regards to the schooling based on Rizal’s perspective alongside online research. To be specific, regarding the educational inequality based on income, background, and friar influence.

We are thankful for this opportunity!

About Our Website

This is a website about the challenges of the education system during the Spanish colonial rule from specific chapters in Jose Rizal's novels, “Noli Me Tángere” and “El Filibusterismo”.
